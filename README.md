# Diabetic Insight - Diabetes Management Website

## Overview
Diabetic Insight is a comprehensive health and wellness website dedicated to helping individuals manage and potentially reverse diabetes through natural methods. The website focuses on providing valuable information, resources, and guidance for people living with diabetes or those looking to prevent it.

## Project Features
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly layout that works across devices
- **Comprehensive Navigation**: Intuitive menu system with dropdown options
- **Educational Content**: Detailed information about diabetes management
- **Multimedia Resources**: Images, videos, and interactive content
- **Contact Page**: Easy communication channel for users

## Structure
The website is organized into several key sections:

### Diet Section
- **Sprouting**: Information about the benefits of eating raw sprouted foods, including how to sprout and which foods to sprout
- **Benefits of Juicing**: Guidance on vegetable and fruit juicing for diabetics, with emphasis on safe choices

### Exercise Section
- **Yoga Exercises**: Specific yoga poses and routines designed for diabetes management
- **Anti-Gravity Exercises**: Home-based resistance exercises to help manage blood sugar

### Meditation Section
- **Dynamic Meditation**: Stress-reduction techniques specifically beneficial for diabetics

## Pages
1. **Home (index.html)**: Main landing page with introduction, key facts, symptoms, prevention, and diagnosis information
2. **Sprouting (Sprouting.html)**: Detailed guide on sprouting benefits and methods
3. **Benefits of Juicing (BenefitsOfJuicing.html)**: Information about juice feasting for diabetes management
4. **Yoga Exercises (YogaExercise.html)**: Various yoga poses beneficial for diabetics
5. **Anti-Gravity Exercises (Antigravity.html)**: Resistance exercises that can be done at home
6. **Dynamic Meditation (DynamicMediation.html)**: Meditation techniques for stress and diabetes management
7. **Contact (contact.html)**: Contact form and information
8. **CSS Files**: Custom styling for each page

## Key Topics Covered
- Diabetes statistics and global impact
- Symptoms recognition and early warning signs

## Target Audience
- Individuals diagnosed with diabetes
