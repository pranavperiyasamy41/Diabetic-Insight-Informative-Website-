Anti-Gravity resistance exercises

Anti-gravity resistance exercises can be performed in the comfort of your home or a hotel room when traveling. You do not need a gym or fancy equipment to perform these exercises. You work against gravity and overcome the resistance imposed by gravity. These exercises will facilitate fat oxidation and glycogen depletion. This is an easy way to cultivate a habit of exercising daily without going to the gym, or it can utilize the extra blood glucose after a heavy meal!
